A few places to visit

  • Alcatraz - Night tours sell out quickly (http://www.alcatrazcruises.com/)
  • Coit Tower
  • Ferry Building
  • Golden Gate Bridge/The Presidio - A popular activity is to rent a bike to go over the bridge and take the Sausalito ferry back
  • Golden Gate Park - Home of the de Young Museum, California Academy of Sciences, Japanese Tea Garden, and Conservatory of Flowers, and bison
  • Land’s End
  • Lombard Street

A few places to eat (disclaimer: collectively we lived in the Mission for 4 years, so this list is skewed towards Mission eats)
  • Bob’s Steakhouse (Downtown) - Shares a kitchen with the Omni Hotel
  • Burma Superstar (Inner Richmond)
  • B*Star (Inner Richmond) - Burma Superstar’s sister restaurant, similar food, shorter wait
  • The Little Chihuahua (Mission) - Fancy burritos
  • Craftsman and Wolves (Mission) - Good pastries
  • El Farolito (Mission ) - No frills burritos
  • Plow (Potrero Hill) - Brunch
  • Emmy’s Spaghetti Shack (Mission) - Yes, it’s spaghetti but it’s really good spaghetti!
  • Lolinda (Mission) - Argentinian steakhouse
  • Pizzeria Delfina (Mission)
  • Rosamunde Sausage Grill (Hayes Valley & Mission)
  • Spruce (Presidio Heights) $$$$ Treat yo'self!
  • Mitchell’s Ice Cream (Mission)
